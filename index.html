  1. Click the "CONTINUE" button to start the game.
  2. You will get only '30s' to give answer.
  3. your score will be '100' if you don't use any block.
  4. If you click a block to reveal some part, then you will get some lesser score.
  5. If you can give the right answer, then click the "CONTINUE" button there to continue.
  6. If you can't give the right answer, then the game will end.
